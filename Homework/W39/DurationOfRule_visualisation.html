Visualisation

to visualise I used ggplot and the geom_line and geom_smooth. I choose the theme minimal which made the background white. I Used labs() to give the visualisation a title, and give names to the x and y axes. When doing so i am given two warnings and they both relate to the fact that Gorm den Gamles start of reign is a missing value (NA) and therefore R hasnt been able to calculate Gorm den Gamles duration of rule and therefore cant visualise it either

kongeraekken %>% 
  ggplot(aes(x = end_of_reign, y = duration_of_rule)) +
  geom_line() +
  geom_smooth() + 
  theme_minimal() +
  labs( title = "Duration of reign among Danish rulers",
        x = "end of reign",
        y = "duration of reign")
